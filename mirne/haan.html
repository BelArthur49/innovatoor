Vision Bearer

Apostle François Nkurunziza is the Vision Bearer and the Presiding Apostle of Harvest Africa Apostolic Network that primarily covers the African continent and the rest of the World.
Harvest Africa is a Vision for Africa providing a wide space for the corporate anointing and converged efforts, skills, expertise and competences of diverse ministers from all walks of life!
He is the founder of MIRNE (International Mission for Restoration of Nations by the Gospel), a ministry born in the context of the aftermath of the 1994 Genocide against Tutsi in Rwanda, and the founder of the Community of Bethel Churches found in Rwanda and abroad.
Apostle Nkurunziza and his wife Bishop Thérèse Umigraneza have been serving the Lord for over thirty years. They have together, four children and three grand kids. In his spiritual journey, Apostle Nkurunziza entered the Monastery in one of the Roman Catholic monastic orders, which he left before his final vows.
Thereafter, he experienced a dramatic conversion in 1983 and was called to full-time ministry shortly after his conversion. He is one of the pioneers of the revival that hit Burundi and other countries in the 80s and 90s. Renowned orator especially on the Biblical Prophecies, he has compiled a very pertinent teaching series entitled "The Signs of the Times". He was trained for ministry at Ecole Missionaire Francophone in France and he holds a B.A. in Law.
