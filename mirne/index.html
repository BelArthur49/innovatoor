MIRNE

MIRNE, an acronym for Mission Internationale pour la Restauration des Nations par l’Evangile, means International Mission for the Restoration of Nations through the Gospel. Its vision embraces all nations.
It serves as a platform for the pooling of anointings, callings, potential, and competencies of God’s servants from all parts of the body of Christ.

Vision

Mirne Vision

To prepare the Bride of Christ for His imminent return and to evangelize the world in obedience to the Great Commission found in
Matthew 28:18–20.

OBJECTIVES

Mirne's Objectives

Create

To create a platform that fosters dynamic exchange among various ministries and their works, encouraging greater mutual knowledge and recognition.

To promote and to serve

To promote the convergence of the torches of the Gospel.
To serve as a catalyst in supporting all evangelistic efforts aimed at hastening the coming of the Lord.

Train

To train and equip every Christian to develop a missionary mindset and be effective in their sphere of influence.
