Mission
CBS Rwanda is an organization whose mission is to contribute to the improvement of psychosocial wellbeing of people affected by the Genocide against Tutsi, a painful past and/or on-going daily stressors, restoring the social fabric, and promoting sustainable peace
Vision
The vision of CBS Rwanda is to promote psychosocial wellbeing in Rwanda through interventions focusing on healing, reconciliation, social cohesion and economic empowerment.

  • Teamwork
