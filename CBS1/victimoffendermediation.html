Victim offender mediation

There is a physical and social distance between (former) inmates and the other community members, especially their victims and their families. A strong need was identified to bridge that distance, in many cases to apologise and ask for forgiveness, before being released.
Among survivors there often is a wish to engage in a sincere conversation with the person who harmed them. Twegeranetrauma-informed mediation creates space for restorative dialogue between its participants to discuss their past experiences and decide on the way forward.
Trauma-informed mediation takes into account that the individuals involved in the mediation may carry deep-seated trauma and therefore seeks to create a safe and supportive environment in which remorse, apology, and forgiveness can take place.
