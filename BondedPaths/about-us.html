About Us

Let’s walk this path together.

Start designing your custom adventure.

Bonded Paths Safari and Tours is a Rwandan-based travel company passionately dedicated to crafting extraordinary, personalized journeys across the heart of East Africa.

We believe the best way to explore Rwanda and Uganda is by forging genuine connections with the wildlife, the land, and the people.

We specialize in meticulous planning and execution, offering seamless safaris that blend adventure and conservation with ultimate comfort and care. Our deep local roots are your assurance of an authentic, insider experience, allowing you to discover the magic of the region beyond the typical tourist trail.

  • Bonded Paths

We are the experts specialized in Gorilla Trekking permits, travel between Rwanda and Uganda, and access to premium, authentic accommodations.

We are your travel partners, not just tour operators. Our warm, personalized approach ensures your journey is built around your unique dreams, pace, and interests.

Our Foundation: Mission, Vision, and Values

Our Mission

Authentic and impactful

To deliver personalized, authentic, and impactful travel experiences across Rwanda and Uganda, connecting people with the region's beauty and resilience while upholding the highest standards of Customer Care and Sustainability..

Our Vision

East Africa’s leading bespoke tour operator

To be recognized as East Africa’s leading bespoke tour operator, the number one choice for travelers seeking excellence, authenticity, and a deep passion for responsible adventure..

Our Core Values

(The Pillars of Bonded Paths)

Authenticity: We create genuine travel experiences rooted in local culture, nature, and heritage, guided by our insider knowledge.
Customer Care: Every journey is designed with meticulous attention to your comfort, safety, and personal fulfillment—our guests are at the heart of everything we do.
Passion: Our love for this region is the driving force behind our work, ensuring we go the extra mile to deliver exceptional and memorable moments.
Responsible Value: We believe high-quality, memorable travel should be managed transparently to deliver the best possible value, ensuring both a premier guest experience and a strong commitment to local economies.
Sustainability: We are committed to responsible, low-impact tourism that protects wildlife (especially the Mountain Gorillas), respects local people, and preserves the environment.
Excellence: We strive to deliver outstanding service and seamless logistics in every detail, setting the highest standard for your African safari.
