Fast and easy way to rent a car

Govarwa is an innovative platform dedicated to car rentals in the heart of Kigali. Our mission is to connect vehicle owners with local residents and tourists looking for reliable, flexible, and tailored transportation solutions.

How it work

Did you just land at the airport and need a car for your stay in Kigali? Check our website to explore a variety of available, reliable, and modern cars. Choose a car that suits your needs and enjoy driving yourself in the safest and cleanest city in Africa. Prefer not to drive? Opt for a car with an experienced driver who will take you anywhere in the country while you sit back and enjoy the breathtaking views of the "Land of a Thousand Hills."

Choose Location

Select from a variety of pick-up locations that best suit your needs, whether it's close to home, work, or airport.

Pick-up Date

Choose the exact date and time for your car pick-up, ensuring that your vehicle is ready when you need it.

Book your Car

Complete your booking with just a just a call, and we'll prepare your vehicle to ensure a hassle-free pick-up.
