Ask A Question

What is OSINT and how does Innovatoor use it?

OSINT (Open Source Intelligence) is collection and analysis of publicly available data. At Innovatoor we use 200+ tools including Maltego and Shodan across competitor analysis, market research, due diligence, and fraud prevention.

What types of businesses benefit from your services?

Startups, established businesses, financial institutions, and any organization seeking data-driven decision-making. Whether you need market research, fraud prevention, or digital transformation, we have a tailored solution.

How does Innovatoor help with market research?

We use advanced OSINT, data scraping, and competitor analysis to gather insights on market trends, consumer behavior, and competitor activities — helping businesses stay ahead across East Africa.

What is due diligence and why is it important?

Due diligence means thoroughly investigating a business or individual before a deal. We provide accurate background checks and risk assessments. 60% of deals fail due to poor due diligence — we prevent that.

How do you prevent scams and fraud?

We use OSINT tools to track fraudulent activities, identify potential threats, and protect your business operations. In 2023, consumers lost over $10B to scams — Innovatoor helps you avoid that.

Can Innovatoor help with lead generation?

Yes. We use verified data sources to build high-quality lead lists, improving sales conversion and growth. We fix the 40% of leads damaged by poor data quality with clean, enriched contacts.

What is company digitalization?

Integrating digital technologies into your operations. We offer website OSINT, social media intelligence, SEO, and automated scraping bots to enhance data collection and smarter decision-making.
