Our Story

Mahanaim's Full Story

Mahanaïm life center is an initiative of the community of Bethel churches in Rwanda (CEBR) dedicated/ aiming to offering services that reflect and uphold the values of the Kingdom of God to all who will require them.
The word 'Mahanaim' is referenced in the Bible, notably within the book of Genesis, chapter 32, verse and verse 2 (Genesis: 32-1-2) It is a narrative about an individual named Jacob, subsequently called Israel during his journey back to his homeland of Canaan from the house of his uncle Laban.
However, Jacob harbored deep concerns in his heart about the impending encounter with his elder brother Esau, from whom he had taken the birthright.
He understood that Esau bore a deep resentment towards him and that their meeting could result in Esau attempting/ wanting to kill him, which prompted Jacob to flee in the first place.

OUR Story

To strengthen His servant Jacob also called Israel, God showed him a vision of angels meeting him. Upon witnessing that, Jacob was filled with joy and named that place 'the camp of God.' So he called that place « Mahanaïm », the camp of God denoting the area where the presence of God manifested itself.
Thus, the initiative was named “Mahanaim Life Center”, embodying the idea that this place will serve as a venue for the reflection of God’s presence and the expression of His Kingdom’s values through the services offered to all in need of them. ???? "Although Mahanaim Life Center offers services similar to those available elsewhere, the underlying values and principles may differ.
Whereas some places aim primarily at generating profit, at Mahanaim life center the core guiding values and goal of this initiative are to offer affordable services primarily through members of Bethel church, even though job opportunities will be extended to individuals regardless of their religious affiliation; that are aligned with the Kingdom of God principles outlined in the epistle of Paul to Galatians 5-22-23.
This implies that peace and tranquility, joy and patience will be evident among those who offer services, along with gentleness, forbearance, and humility. Therefore, along with numerous other values intrinsic to the Kingdom of God, the fruits of the Holy Spirit will be a defining feature of all those providing service in the name of the Lord Jesus Christ.
As such, Mahanaim Life Center stands as an initiative of the Kingdom of God, incorporating various services in such a way that whoever comes will find a range of offerings living up to the name “Mahanaim life center” designed to be like a small village where every good thing needed can be found. This place was dedicated with prayers that God would reveal Himself through this project to all who visit, so that they may not only be assisted but also come to understand/know the salvation of our God. We therefore extend our invitation to all who seek various services to visit Mahanaim Life Center, where they will have the opportunity to experience the unique nature of Kingdom-driven services, distinguishing them from those available elsewhere. Accordingly, the uniqueness and values observed here are unparalleled, found solely in a project carried by the power of the Kingdom of God.
Thank you, and may The Lord Jesus bless you.
