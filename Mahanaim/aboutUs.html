Our Story

Mahanaim's Full Story

Mahanaïm Life Center is a project of the Community of Eglise Bethel Churches in Rwanda (CEBR) that offers services deeply rooted in the values of the Kingdom of God. Drawing inspiration from the biblical story of Jacob, who named a place "Mahanaïm" after encountering God's angels, the center is dedicated to being a place where God's presence is reflected in everything it does. While providing services similar to those found elsewhere, Mahanaïm Life Center is unique in its commitment to Kingdom-driven principles such as peace, joy, patience, and humility, as outlined in Galatians 5:22-23. The center prioritizes offering affordable services that are guided by these values, with the goal of making a positive impact on all who seek assistance. Designed to be like a "village" where every good thing can be found, Mahanaïm Life Center invites visitors to experience its distinctive approach.









Vision Bearer

Dr. Apôtre Nkurunziza François is the visionary leader and founder of the Community of Eglise Bethel Churches in Rwanda. His journey of faith began in 1983 when he was saved, a pivotal moment that would shape his life's mission. Inspired by a deep sense of purpose and a calling to serve, Dr. Nkurunziza established the Bethel Churches in 1998, which have since grown into a vibrant spiritual community. He holds a doctorate, reflecting his commitment to both spiritual and academic excellence. Beyond his role as a spiritual leader, Dr. Nkurunziza is a devoted family man. He is married to Umugiraneza Thérèse, and together they have been blessed with four children and two grandchildren. Dr. Nkurunziza's leadership and vision continue to inspire and guide the Bethel community, making a profound impact on the lives of many across Rwanda.
