Our Story

Every Stone Tells a Story

Mahanaim Life Center is a premier event venue nestled within the picturesque landscape of Rwanda. Owned and operated by the Community of Bethel Churches, this exceptional facility has become a sought-after destination for weddings, corporate events, and other special occasions.
Established in 2024, Mahanaim Life Center was envisioned as a space that could cater to the diverse needs of the community. With its spacious tent, elegant garden, and comfortable guest house, the center quickly gained popularity as a versatile venue.
